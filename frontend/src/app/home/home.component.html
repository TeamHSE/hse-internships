Welcome to Internship Finder

Find internships, manage events, and connect with companies for future collaborations. This platform is designed to facilitate internship searches, streamline event organization by companies, and foster meaningful connections between students and employers.

Get Started

Killing Feature

Personalized Event Notifications

Our standout feature is the ability to notify students via Telegram bot or email (student's choice) about new events from companies (internships, workshops, etc.). Each company event can be tagged using a tag cloud. Additionally, users can customize their notification preferences by selecting specific tags (e.g., backend, frontend, CS, internship, etc.) in their profile. When a company adds new events, students who have chosen similar tags receive notifications on their preferred communication channels. This approach ensures that students receive notifications only for events that match their interests, thereby increasing student engagement.

Key Features

For Students

Students can customize their experience by modifying interest tags, selecting preferred notification methods, and reviewing their interaction history.

For Project Office

Office administrators create new events, adjust tag categories, edit event details, and monitor event responses.

For Employers

Employers can create new events, update event details, access event listings, and review responses from participants.
